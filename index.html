My Services

  • Software development

    I specialize in designing and creating software and is a driver of all aspects of software development from refining and designing and development and maintanance. I have a keen eye on balancing time, scope and technical ambition.
    Architecture and development is best done in close collaboration and driven by business needs, this ensures feasibility of proposed architecture and commitment from the architect to solving real challenges for the development teams.
    With me on the team you will get a senior developer and architect that is a team player and an excellent communicator. I emphasise on communication because I find good communcation crucial to the collaboration within a team and with stakeholders in the rest of the organization.

  • Architecture Review

    Independant and open review of architecture and over all state of an existing system. We will use our experience to pin point strengths and weaknesses in your system, and deliver clear and tangible actions to move your system/product forward. We will review the system from a number of viewpoints, namely System Architecture, Code Review (quality and maintainability) and Security and asses the state of each of these relative to the problem it needs to solve.
    The findings of the architecture review is presented to the client on a meeting and handed over in written form after the presentation.

  • Second opinion

    If you need a second opinion on an important project or you are looking into changing a system in your IT landscape, then I can assist you with insights from prior experiences to help you stay on track.

  • Domain driven design Workshop

    A two day workshop where you clarify your over all project needs and design to hit the ground running when morphing into feature development.
